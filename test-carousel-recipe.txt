Classic Coq au Vin Recipe

This traditional French braising technique transforms chicken into a rich, wine-infused masterpiece.

Ingredients:
- 4 chicken thighs and legs
- 1 cup Burgundy red wine for deglazing
- 2 cups chicken stock
- 4 oz smoked bacon, diced
- 12 pearl onions, peeled
- 8 oz mushrooms, quartered
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- Fresh thyme and bay leaves for bouquet garni
- 2 tbsp flour for dredging
- Salt and pepper
- Butter for sautéing

Instructions:
1. Season chicken and dredge in flour
2. Sear chicken until golden in a Dutch oven
3. Remove chicken and sauté bacon until crispy
4. Add pearl onions and caramelize them
5. Deglaze with red wine, scraping up fond
6. Return chicken with stock and bouquet garni
7. Braise in oven at 325°F for 90 minutes
8. Add mushrooms in the last 30 minutes
9. Finish with a butter enrichment (monter au beurre)
10. Garnish with fresh parsley

This dish showcases classic French techniques like braising, deglazing, and creating a rich sauce through reduction.
