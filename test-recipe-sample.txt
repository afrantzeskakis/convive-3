French Coq au Vin

Ingredients:
- 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
- 4 slices bacon, chopped
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 carrots, sliced
- 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 bottle red wine (Burgundy preferred)
- 2 bay leaves
- Fresh thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tbsp flour
- 2 tbsp butter

Instructions:
1. Cook bacon until crispy, remove and set aside
2. Season chicken with salt and pepper, brown in bacon fat
3. Remove chicken, saut√© onions and carrots
4. Add garlic and mushrooms, cook 3 minutes
5. Sprinkle flour, cook 1 minute
6. Add wine, bay leaves, and thyme
7. Return chicken and bacon to pot
8. Simmer covered for 45 minutes
9. Finish with butter before serving
